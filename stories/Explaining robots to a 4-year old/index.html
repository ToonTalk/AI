The Adventure of Robbie the Robot

Once she had her design, Dr. Mia started gathering parts. She used metal for his body, rubber for his tires, and special lights for his eyes. She worked day and night, turning, screwing, and welding until one day... Robbie was complete!

Now that Robbie was built, he needed to learn. Dr. Mia taught him how to speak, "Hello, I'm Robbie!" Robbie chirped happily. With every passing day, Robbie became smarter and more helpful.

Finally, it was time for Robbie to meet the world. Dr. Mia took him to a park where kids were playing. Robbie was excited! From that day on, Robbie became a beloved member of the city. Everywhere he went, he made new friends and spread joy. And all the kids, especially the 4-year-olds, loved their shiny robot friend who could play, sing, and help in so many ways.
