Zara and the Magic of Numbers That Stand Alone

In the time when mammoths roamed the earth, a clever cave girl named Zara loved to play with the smooth pebbles she collected from the river. One sunny morning outside her family's cave, she decided to arrange her seven favorite pebbles into a perfect rectangle. She tried making rows of two, but one pebble was left over. She tried rows of three, but again, pebbles remained alone. No matter how hard she tried, seven pebbles would never form a neat rectangle. 'These pebbles are stubborn!' she laughed, not knowing she had discovered something magical about the number seven.
