A Whisper in the Dark

The hour after midnight...

Dear Diary,

The cold stone of the palace steps still haunts my memory, as does the echoing chime of the clock striking twelve. The magic tore itself away from me in shreds of moonlight and spun glass, leaving only the chill of reality in its wake.

He held me as if the rest of the room, the rest of the kingdom, was completely empty. For a few fleeting hours, I wasn't soot and cinders; I was entirely myself, seen in the reflection of his eyes. But what happens when the enchantment breaks? I fled before the illusion could shatter, leaving behind nothing but a solitary glass slipper on the stairs.

I sit by the hearth now, tracing the ashes, wondering if it was all merely a dream born of desperation. If he truly saw the girl beneath the glamour, would he search for me? Or am I destined to fade away, just another forgotten phantom of the midnight hour?

Are You Searching For Me?

A secret kept until the slipper fits.
