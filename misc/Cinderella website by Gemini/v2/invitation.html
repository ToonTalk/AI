Royal Decree

By Order of His Majesty, the King

All eligible maidens of the kingdom are hereby commanded to attend a Royal Ball at the Palace, held in honor of His Royal Highness, the Prince.

Festivities shall commence precisely at dusk.
Formal attire is strictly required.
