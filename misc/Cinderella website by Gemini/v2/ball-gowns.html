The Royal Ball Gowns

A journey from humble beginnings to magical transformations

The Mice's Masterpiece

Before the magic of the Fairy Godmother, there was the magic of true friendship. Crafted with love, dedication, and a few cleverly gathered materials, this lovely pink dress was originally worn by Cinderella's beloved mother.

Jaq, Gus, the bluebirds, and the rest of the loyal animal companions worked tirelessly to update the gown. They adorned it with a beautiful white sash and delicate beads, creating a vision of rosy elegance. Though its time at the ball was not meant to be, the heart and soul woven into this dress make it a timeless masterpiece of devotion.

The Midnight Silver Gown

Born from a shower of starlight and the wave of a magic wand, the Midnight Silver Gown is the pinnacle of enchanted fashion. The Fairy Godmother spun this breathtaking creation out of pure magic, tailoring it perfectly to Cinderella's grace.

The gown features a voluminous, shimmering skirt that captures the essence of the night sky, accented by elegant draping and a neckline that sparkles like diamonds in the moonlight. Paired perfectly with the legendary glass slippers, this majestic silver dress ensured that Cinderella was the absolute center of attention the moment she stepped into the royal palace.
