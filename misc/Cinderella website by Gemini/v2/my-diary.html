My Secret Heart

✧ Hidden Pages of a Midnight Dreamer ✧
A quiet midnight, long after the embers faded
Dear Diary,

My hands are so raw tonight. I spent hours scrubbing the cold, stone floors of the grand hall until they mirrored the ceiling, yet it still wasn't enough for Stepmother. The lye burns my skin, but I try not to let it reach my heart.

Today was especially difficult. Drizella spilled her morning tea on purpose, just to watch me wipe it up, and Anastasia laughed as she tracked mud across the parlor I had just swept. They call me 'Cinderwench'. The ashes from the hearth cling to my hair and smudge my cheeks, and sometimes, when I catch my reflection in the wash basin, I hardly recognize myself beneath the soot.

But they don't know my secret. When the house finally falls silent and I retreat to my drafty attic, I sit by the tiny window and gaze out at the palace. It stands so proud against the midnight sky, bathed in silver starlight. In the quiet of the night, I close my eyes and I am no longer a servant in my own home. I imagine I am wearing a gown spun from the night sky itself, drifting across a grand ballroom to the most beautiful music.

They can make me sweep the cinders, they can mock my ragged clothes, but they cannot chain my spirit. As long as I have my dreams, I am free. I must believe that somewhere, somehow, there is a better life waiting for me. I have to keep believing.

Yours in hope,
Cinderella
