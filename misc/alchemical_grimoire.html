Alchemical Grimoire
Records of Experimental Brews

Patient: Duchess Gigglesworth of the Chuckling Valley
Affliction: Her laughter spontaneously transmutes into a flock of disgruntled, miniature marmots
Recipe: Combos of "marmot food pellets " and "booing"
Method: Sing a lullaby until it coalesces
The The Grumblefeed Guffaw Suppressant
Brewing Result: Upon brewing, the mixture of pellets and captured 'booing' sounds coalesces into a shimmering, grey-green liquid that softly hums a discordant, low note. When consumed, a fleeting, almost transparent image of a single, politely waving marmot appears above the Duchess's head, then vanishes.
Clinical Efficacy: The 'marmot food pellets' act as a spiritual satiation for the nascent marmot essence within her laughter, effectively 'feeding' the marmots before they fully manifest. Concurrently, the 'booing' ingredient, transmuted into a powerful sonic dampener by the lullaby method, internally cancels out the specific frequency of her laughter responsible for the physical transmutation. Consequently, her laughter ceases to produce tangible marmots.
Unintended Consequence: While her laughter is marmot-free, the residual 'booing' energy causes her to involuntarily emit a tiny, polite "boo" whenever she hears a particularly loud or obnoxious sound. Additionally, the 'marmot food pellet' essence bestows upon her an inexplicable, delightful urge to collect shiny objects and meticulously hoard them in small, hidden nooks around her castle.
Efficacy: 80% Toxicity: 70%
