The timeline

because everyone keeps asking
Five chapters.
Before, The Spell, The Ball, The Escape, After. Each one includes… receipts.
If you’re reading this: yes, I ran.
Hover me (if you want to feel stress).
When you hover, it ticks louder and the page darkens slightly.
Before
Laundry. Ashes. Sewing dreams in tiny stolen moments.
INVITATION
Scanned-looking royal seal. Extremely inconvenient date/time. No RSVP link.
Context
The household was not… supportive. Let’s keep it at that.
The Spell
Pumpkin coach. Footwear that doubles as a liability waiver.
COACH PERMIT
Issued by: “Fairy Godmother, LLC”. Expires at midnight. Obviously.
Practical note
If your outfit has an expiration time, add a timer. This site is that timer.
The Ball
Chandeliers. Music. Dancing with someone who listens like it’s a superpower.
BALLROOM LOG
Unverified rumor: the prince can’t dance. Verified: he tries anyway.
Tiny detail
I did not know you could breathe in a corset. Still not sure I did.
The Escape
11:59. Stairs. A shoe turning into a plot device.
LEFT SHOE INCIDENT
Location: staircase. Time: too close to midnight. Emotion: sprint.
Lesson
Glass is not a running shoe material. No one asked me. No one consulted physics.
After
Back to normal. Except now there’s a royal search party and one missing slipper.
PUBLIC NOTICE
“Owner of slipper requested.” I’m sure this won’t be dramatic.
This website
Part scrapbook. Part evidence locker. Part preemptive stepmother defense.
