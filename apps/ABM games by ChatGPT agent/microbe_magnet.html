About Microbe Magnet

In this simulation you control an invisible magnetic field that influences the movement of microscopic organisms. Move your mouse over the canvas to attract the microbes toward your pointer. When the field is active (click and hold), the microbes accelerate toward the cursor, forming swirling patterns. Release the mouse to let them drift freely. Over time, microbes divide, causing the swarm to grow and take on new forms. Try to see what shapes you can coax out of the chaos.
