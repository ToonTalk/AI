Agent‑Based Simulation Games

Explore a collection of imaginative, interactive simulations built around simple rules that lead to complex behaviours.

Microbe Magnet
Harness a magnetic field to coax microbes into swirling patterns and avoid predators.
 Chaotic City Shuffle
Manipulate traffic flow by placing obstacles and watch commuters adapt (or not).
 Neural Swarm Orchestra
Connect neurons to shape emergent musical patterns from a firing network.
 Pollinator Predator Garden
Plant flowers and balance pollinators and predators in a miniature ecosystem.
 Belief Cascade
Drop fact or rumour bombs into a social network and observe how opinions shift.
 Nanobot Maze Escape
Watch nanobots follow simple rules to escape a shifting maze.
 Dream Herd Migration
Guide migrating animals by planting dream cues that alter their internal compass.
