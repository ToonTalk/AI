About Neural Swarm Orchestra

This interactive experiment turns a simple neural network into a musical instrument. Each node in the network accumulates charge over time and fires when its threshold is reached. When a node fires, it sends a pulse to its neighbours and produces a short tone. The collective firing patterns generate emergent rhythms and melodies.

You can influence the network by clicking on nodes: each click randomly rewires one of the nodeâ€™s connections, adding or removing a link to another node. More connections result in more synchronized firing; fewer connections produce sparser patterns. Explore how structure affects sound.
