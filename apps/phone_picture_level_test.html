How to use

  1. Tap Enable Sensors.
  2. Place the long edge of the phone along the top or bottom edge of the picture frame.
  3. Adjust the picture until the bubble sits in the center and the reading is near 0.0°.
  4. If your phone case or frame edge is odd, hold the phone in your preferred position and tap Set current as level.
