About Pollinator Predator Garden

Welcome to a tiny ecosystem where pollinators and predators interact. In this simulation you can plant flowers by clicking on the garden. Bees wander randomly in search of blossoms; when they find one, they gather nectar and increase the flower’s pollination counter. Flowers with high pollination counts may cause new blooms to appear nearby.

Spiders roam the garden looking for bees. If a spider catches a bee, the bee is removed (and will respawn elsewhere). Balance the number of flowers so that there’s plenty of nectar to attract bees without letting predators wipe them out. There is no final goal; enjoy watching the dynamic interactions unfold.
