Cone forward mode
Geodesic follows a straight line on the cone's unwrapped sector. Bearing keeps the same local bearing relative to guide curves.
In geodesic mode, the cone is treated as if you cut it open into a flat sector, drew a straight line there, and wrapped that line back onto the cone. So one long forward move does not usually stay on a guide circle. Bearing mode does.
