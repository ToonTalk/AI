Fibonacci Sequence Calculator

What is the Fibonacci Sequence?

The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number after the first two is the sum of the previous two.

How does this calculator work?

This calculator allows you to input the number of terms you want to compute in the Fibonacci sequence. When you click the 'Calculate Fibonacci' button, the calculator generates the corresponding Fibonacci sequence up to the specified number of terms and displays the result.
