How this was made

This animation is a live blend of two little Logo turtle programs — instructions telling a turtle to go forward and turn. What you see is shape A mixing into shape B step by step, and it can keep going past either end (that is extrapolation). Open it below to drag, extrapolate, and remix.

Shape A
setpencolour orange
repeat 5 [forward 92 right 144 forward 92 left 72]
Shape B
setpencolour cyan
repeat 36 [forward 18 right 10]
