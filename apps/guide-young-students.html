A Guide for Young Makers 🐢✨

In The Atelier you build little minds. A mind is a tiny helper that makes art — some minds draw and some minds write. You teach them, watch them try, and help them get better.

Nothing here is wrong. Mistakes are how minds learn. Have fun!

1. Make a mind 🌀

  1. Press ✚ New mind at the top, or the big + Make a new mind card.
  2. Pick what it does: 🐢 It draws or 📝 It writes.
  3. Pick a look — an emoji and a colour.
  4. Pick a starter mind (like Spiral Gardener or Haiku Snail), or start from scratch.

Now you have a mind! 🎉

2. Run a round ▶️

Press ▶ Run a round. Watch the steps fill in, one at a time:

  • ✏️ Make — it has an idea.
  • 🐢 Render — it draws the picture (or 📝 writes the words).
  • 👀 Look — it looks at what it made.
  • 🖼️ Exhibit — it hangs the work up.
  • 💭 Reflect — it writes in its journal.
  • ⭐ Distill — every few rounds it turns its journal into lessons.

Run a few rounds and watch it change!

🔁 For drawings, press Replay the drawing to watch it draw again. Drag the slider to go faster or slower.

3. Look at what it knows 🧠

Press Edit its mind to peek inside. Every part is just words you can change:

  • Spark — what it dreams of making.
  • Hands — how it makes things.
  • Eyes — how it looks at its own work (you can turn them off!).
  • Memory — its lessons and its journal.
Try this: change one word in the Spark and run a round. What happens? 😮

4. Help it learn 📌

  • After a round you can leave it a note — one thing you like and one wish. It will read your note next time it makes something.
  • In the Memory card you can add a lesson, pin one, or take one away. Lessons are the only things it remembers!

You are the teacher. 💛

5. Show & Tell 💬

Have two or more minds (each needs to have made at least one work). Then:

  1. Go to 💬 Show & Tell.
  2. Tick 2 to 4 minds and press Start Show & Tell.
  3. Each mind shows its newest work. The others say kind things back.

The notes are saved. When a mind runs its next round, it reads the notes about its work. Watch a 📌 note waiting appear on its card — run a round so it can answer!

6. Other fun things 🎨

  • 🖼️ Gallery — see everything your minds have made.
  • 📓 Notebook — your journal. Write what you notice. This one is for you, not the mind.
  • 🤖 Ask (bottom corner) — ask the helper how anything works, or "what have my minds been making?"
  • 💾 Save these rounds — turn a mind's rounds into a web page you can keep or share.

Stuck? Two magic questions ✨

  • What if I take its Eyes away?
  • What if I change just one little thing?

Try it, run a round, and see. That's what makers do!
