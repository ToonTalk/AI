Symbiotic Micro-Behavior Evolution
Evolution by combination, not crossover
Configure an evolutionary experiment over 2-turtle organisms. Each organism's two ensembles maintain their identity — there is no genetic crossover. Novelty arises through COUPLE (new inter-turtle interaction behaviors), DUPLICATE (gene duplication within an ensemble), and TUNE (slow specialization).
