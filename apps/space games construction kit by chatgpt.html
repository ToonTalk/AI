How this prototype works

The team does not just dump resources into your inventory. Specialists give ideas. The programmer converts sufficiently clear ideas into behaviour cards. That handoff is deliberate: scientist/designer first, programmer second.

  • Click a teammate to get an immediate greeting and real question choices.
  • Use the Resources panel to attach behaviour cards to the selected object.
  • You can now attach any unlocked behaviour to any object. Recommended attachments are marked, but mistakes are allowed because they can produce useful debugging conversations.
  • For Lunar Lander vertical motion: start the Lunar brief, ask the scientist why vertical position matters, then ask the programmer to create the behaviour card.
